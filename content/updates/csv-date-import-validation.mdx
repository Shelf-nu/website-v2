When importing assets via CSV, **date custom fields** (`cf:…,type:date`) are now strictly validated against the `YYYY-MM-DD` format. Previously, a day-first dash format such as `03-04-2026` could be silently stored as a completely wrong date (like `1908-10-16`), and impossible dates such as `2026-02-31` were quietly rolled over — corrupting sorting, filtering, reports, and reminders without any warning.

Now any value that isn't a valid `YYYY-MM-DD` date **stops the import with a clear error**, so you can correct the column and re-import with confidence that your dates are stored exactly as intended. Valid ISO dates continue to import exactly as before.
