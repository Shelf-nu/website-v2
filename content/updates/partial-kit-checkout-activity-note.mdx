---
title: "Clearer Activity Log for Partial Kit Check-outs"
description: "Checking out or in only some assets of a kit now names those assets in the booking activity log, instead of leaving an empty entry."
date: "2026-06-23"
image: "/images/updates/partial-check-ins.jpg"
layout: "update"
---

When you check out or check in only **some** of a kit's assets — without taking the whole kit — the booking activity log now names each individual asset that was processed. Previously, a partial action that touched only individual kit assets could record an entry with no item names at all (for example, "performed a partial check-out: ."), making it hard to see what actually moved.

This applies to partial check-outs and check-ins on both web and the Shelf Companion app, so booking history stays accurate and complete no matter how you process your assets.
