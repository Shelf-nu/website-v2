The bulk **Actions** menu on a booking's item list — **Check out**, **Check in**, and **Remove** — is now context-aware, so it only ever offers what your current selection can actually do.

If none of the selected items are checked out, **Check in** is greyed out with a short explanation ("None of the selected items are checked out, so there's nothing to check in"). If every selected item is already checked out, **Check out** is greyed out the same way. Pick a mix of eligible and ineligible items and the action stays available, runs on just the eligible ones, and tells you how many were skipped — no more selecting a batch only to have the dialog quietly act on a fraction of it. The menu now also follows the real lifecycle order (Check out → Check in → Remove), and the selection clears automatically once a batch completes, so you can move straight on to the next group.
