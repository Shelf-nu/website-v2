Finding the right location just got faster. The **Locations** index now includes a **Sort by** dropdown with three options:

- **Date created** (the default, newest first)
- **Name** (alphabetical)
- **Number of assets** (handy for spotting your busiest — or emptiest — locations)

Search is broader too. The locations search box previously matched only the location name; it now also searches each location's **description** and **address**, all case-insensitive. So you can jump straight to a site by typing part of its street address or a note you saved in its description, not just its exact name.

It's a small change that adds up quickly once you're managing more than a handful of rooms, buildings, or job sites.
