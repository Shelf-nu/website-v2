---
title: "Annual Plan Pricing Displays Correctly"
description: "Fixed: the in-app plan selection screen was showing incorrect prices for annual subscriptions. Annual plans now display the correct yearly total with a /yr suffix."
date: "2026-04-16"
layout: "update"
---

The plan selection screen was displaying incorrect prices for annual billing. A calculation error caused the shown price to be about one-tenth of the actual annual amount — for example, a $370/yr plan would appear as roughly $37, which is neither the correct annual total nor a meaningful monthly figure. This has been fixed.

Annual plans now show the correct total annual price with a `/yr` suffix. Monthly plans continue to show `/mo`. The footnote text was also cleaned up to simply read "Billed annually" or "Billed monthly."

No actual plan prices changed — only the display was wrong. If you were evaluating annual billing and the numbers looked unusually low, those figures were incorrect. What you see now is what you will actually be charged.
