---
title: "Annual Plan Pricing Now Displays Correctly"
description: "Fixed: the plan selection screen was showing incorrect prices for annual subscriptions. Annual plans now show the correct yearly total with a /yr suffix."
date: "2026-04-14"
layout: "update"
---

The in-app plan selection screen was displaying incorrect prices for annual billing. A calculation error caused yearly prices to appear at a fraction of their actual value — for example, showing a price that was far lower than the actual annual charge. This has been fixed.

Annual plans now show the correct total annual price with a `/yr` suffix (for example, $370/yr for the Team plan billed annually). A separate typo fix corrects "Billed montly" to "Billed monthly" for month-to-month plans.

No actual plan prices changed — only the way they were displayed was incorrect. If you evaluated annual pricing in the app before this fix and saw numbers that seemed unusually low, those figures were wrong. The prices shown now reflect what you will actually be charged.
