Select a batch of assets, kits, bookings, or locations, run a bulk action like **Update location**, **Assign tags**, or **Delete**, and Shelf now clears your selection automatically once it succeeds. No more hunting for "unselect all" before you start the next group — the checkboxes reset themselves and you can pick your next batch right away.

This applies across the board: every bulk action dialog on your index and list pages now behaves the same way. The two sensible exceptions stay put — read-only operations like exporting data or downloading QR codes keep your selection (they don't change anything), and the **Add to booking** and **Add to audit** panels keep it too, so you can add the same items to another booking or audit without re-selecting them.
