title: "SSO Users Now Land in the Right Workspace"
description: "Personal workspaces are now hidden for SSO users, ensuring they always see and operate within their assigned team workspaces."

SSO users no longer see their personal workspace anywhere in Shelf — the workspace switcher, account settings, and organization context now only show the team workspaces assigned by their administrator. This eliminates the confusion of SSO-managed users accidentally working in a personal workspace instead of their organizational one.

For users who log in via SSO and have not yet been assigned to a team workspace, Shelf now shows a clear **"No workspace assigned"** page instead of an error or a broken state. The page includes instructions to contact their IT administrator, and automatically redirects them once access is granted.

This is particularly useful for organizations onboarding new employees via SSO — users get a clear, actionable message rather than an empty or confusing interface.
