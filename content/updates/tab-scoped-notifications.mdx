If you work with multiple Shelf tabs open at the same time, you may have noticed that completing any action — editing an asset, checking in equipment, updating a booking — would flash a success notification in **every** open tab at once.

This is now fixed. Toast notifications are scoped to the tab that triggered the action. If you update an asset in one tab, only that tab shows the confirmation. Your other tabs stay quiet.

Notifications from background jobs (scheduled reminders, automated tasks) continue to appear across all tabs, as expected.
