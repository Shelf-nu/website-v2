If your workspace was set to a non-USD currency, values across reports were still rendering with a `$` and US number formatting. That's fixed.

Every monetary value — the hero KPIs and table cells in **Asset Inventory**, **Idle Assets**, **Asset Distribution**, and **Asset Utilization**, plus the PDF export — now uses the currency and locale configured on your workspace. Switching your workspace currency to GBP, EUR, JPY, or anything else is reflected immediately on the report and in any PDF you download.
