The Barcodes add-on is now fully self-service. Workspace **owners** can activate alternative barcode support (Code128, Code39, Data Matrix, EAN-13) directly from the asset form — no need to contact sales or wait for manual provisioning.

**New workflow:** Open any asset, find the Barcodes section, and click to start a **7-day free trial**. If your workspace has already used the trial, you can subscribe on a monthly or yearly basis and start immediately via Stripe checkout.

Non-owner team members who don't yet have barcodes enabled will see a prompt to ask their workspace owner to activate the add-on.
