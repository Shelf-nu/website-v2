Completed audits now have a full lifecycle. After an audit is finished — or cancelled — admins and owners can archive it to remove it from the default audit list.

Archiving keeps your audit list focused on active work without permanently deleting historical records. Archived audits remain accessible at any time by filtering for the **Archived** status. Archived audits are read-only: they cannot be edited, scanned, completed, or cancelled after archiving.

To archive an audit, open it from the audit list, click the actions menu (⋯), and select **Archive**. A confirmation dialog prevents accidental archiving. The action is available on any audit with a **Completed** or **Cancelled** status and requires an Admin or Owner role.
