Dates in report tables were always rendered in US format (`Mar 11, 2026`), regardless of your workspace locale. Teams on en-GB, fr-FR, de-DE, ja-JP, and other locales now see dates formatted the way they expect throughout report tables.

This is the date-formatting companion to [last week's currency fix](/updates/reports-currency-locale-fix) — report output now respects both the currency and the date conventions configured for your workspace.
