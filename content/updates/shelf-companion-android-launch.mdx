Shelf Companion is now live on [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.shelf.companion) for Android — joining the [iPhone app](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6765639874) that launched in May. Free with any Shelf account, including the free Personal tier.

## What it does

- **Scan Shelf QR codes and registered barcodes** — Code 128, Code 39, EAN-13, DataMatrix, and external QR — with the phone camera, jump straight to the asset.
- **View the full asset detail** — image, status, category, location, custody, and recent activity.
- **Update asset location** and **assign or release custody** right from the floor.
- **Run audits live** — walk, scan, see found / expected / unexpected counts update in real time, complete the audit when done.
- **Booking check-in and check-out** — process scans for a booking from the gear room.
- **Multi-workspace** — switch organizations from Settings; the app respects each workspace's role-based access.

## What stays on the web today

- Adding notes and photos to an individual audit scan (per-scan evidence capture is web-only for now; on the roadmap for a future release).
- Linking a QR code to an asset or creating a new asset from an unlinked code (the app shows an informational message; do the linking in the web app for now).
- Workspace setup, billing, configuration, custom fields, user/role management, integrations, and full reporting.

## Get the app

Sign in with the credentials you already use at [app.shelf.nu](https://app.shelf.nu). Get Shelf Companion on [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.shelf.companion) for Android, or the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6765639874) for iPhone.
