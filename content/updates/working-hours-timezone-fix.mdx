---
title: "Working Hours Override Dates Now Apply on the Correct Day"
description: "Fixed a timezone issue that caused closed-day overrides to block bookings on the wrong day for users in western time zones."
date: "2026-04-28"
image: "/images/updates/working-hours-timezone-fix.jpg"
layout: "update"
---

If your workspace uses date overrides in Working Hours settings (e.g., holidays or closed days), those overrides are now applied on the exact calendar date you specified — regardless of your time zone.

Previously, users located west of UTC (any timezone in the Americas) could experience a false "This date is closed" error when trying to book on the day *before* a configured closed date. For example, setting April 24 as a closed day would incorrectly block bookings on April 23 for teams in Central or Pacific time. The override dates are now compared as absolute calendar days, so a closed April 24 means April 24 — no earlier, no later.

No action is needed. The fix applies automatically to all workspaces with working hours enabled.
