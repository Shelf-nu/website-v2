Reports is now live in Shelf. A new **Reports** entry in the sidebar opens a grid of ten ready-to-run reports — no SQL, no separate BI tool, no exporting bookings to a spreadsheet to write formulas.

The first batch covers the questions operations managers get asked most often:

**Booking reports** — Booking Compliance (on-time checkouts and late returns), Top Booked Assets, Monthly Booking Trends, and a live Overdue Items list.

**Asset reports** — Asset Inventory (snapshot for audits and board reports), Asset Activity Summary, Asset Utilization, Idle Assets, and Asset Distribution by category and location.

**Custody reports** — Custody Snapshot, the live answer to "who has what right now?"

Time-based reports include a **timeframe picker** for last 7 days / 30 days / quarter / custom range. Each report has filters that match its data — status and custodian for compliance, category and location for utilization, and so on. Filters are encoded in the URL, so a specific view of a report can be bookmarked or shared.

Every report exports to **CSV**. Reports with charts also download as **PDF** — useful for board packs, audit trails, and distribution outside the workspace.

This is v1 of an ongoing reporting build. More reports — kit utilization, custom field analytics, and state-over-time trends — are on the roadmap.
