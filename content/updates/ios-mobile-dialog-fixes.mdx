A small but important sweep of iOS Safari fixes for anyone using Shelf on an iPhone or iPad.

The **Confirm button** in the asset and kit pickers is now reachable on iPhone — previously it could sit below the URL bar with no way to scroll to it, blocking the "Manage assets" flow on mobile. **Dropdowns** in team invites, asset filters, and booking selectors now register taps reliably on iPad and iPhone. And **image and PDF previews** now use the full visible viewport instead of being clipped, while toast notifications clear the home indicator on iPhones that have one. No action needed — the fixes apply automatically.
