Bookings now check out the way real handoffs actually happen — in stages. Until now a reserved booking was checked out all at once: one **Check Out** action flipped every item to "Checked out" together. That's fine when the whole list leaves at the same moment, but gear often goes out piece by piece as it's collected or located.

You can now check out a booking's items **one at a time** by scanning — pick **Scan to check out** from the Check Out dropdown — or **in bulk** by selecting rows and choosing **Actions > Check out selected items**. The booking becomes **Ongoing** on the very first item, while anything you haven't reached yet stays reserved for the booking and available to verify. New **"Checked out on"** and **"Checked out by"** columns record exactly when each item left and who handed it over.

To keep the whole journey visible, the booking's progress bar is now a segmented **Booked / Checked out / Returned** lifecycle bar. It reads **"Check-out progress"** while items are still going out, then switches to **"Check-in progress"** once everything has left and you're tracking returns — and it honors the *count each kit as a single unit* setting in both phases.

This is the checkout counterpart to [partial check-ins](/knowledge-base/partial-check-ins-efficiently-handle-incomplete-returns), and the two work together: hand gear out in stages, and check it back in in stages. Shelf keeps your records honest in the in-between state too — items that were never checked out are never logged as returned.
