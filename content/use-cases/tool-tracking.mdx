# Tool Tracking

Field teams, construction crews, and technicians use tools across constantly changing environments. Shelf provides visibility, accountability, and simple scanning workflows suited for fast-paced operations.

---

## Tool Tracking Challenges

Teams often experience:

- missing tools
- unclear responsibility
- tools left on job sites
- no record of technician transfers
- trucks acting as moving storage
- difficulty tracking accessories and consumables
