# Equipment Management for Consultancies

Consultancies run on a pool of expensive, shared instruments — sound level meters, monitors, gauges, samplers, total stations — that move between staff, offices, and client sites every week. Two things have to be true at all times: the right instrument is **available** when a job needs it, and it's **in calibration** when it's used. Miss either and the work stalls or has to be redone.

Shelf brings booking, calibration tracking, and a complete audit trail into one place, so a small ops team can run a multi-office instrument pool without spreadsheets or sign-out sheets.

---

## The Problems Consultancies Hit

- **Double-booked instruments**: Two jobs need the same meter on the same day and nobody finds out until the morning of.
- **Kit stuck at the wrong office**: Equipment lives across several offices with no shared view of what's where.
- **Calibration surprises**: An instrument goes out of calibration mid-project, and the readings are suddenly in question.
- **No chain of custody**: When a unit comes back damaged, there's no record of who had it last.
- **Audit scramble**: A client or accreditation body asks for calibration and usage records, and the team rebuilds them by hand.

## How Shelf Solves It

### Book Shared Instruments Across Offices

Staff reserve the instruments a job needs and see real-time availability, so two projects never claim the same unit. [Bookings](/features/bookings) prevent double-bookings and show what's free, what's out, and when it's due back.

### Keep Every Office's Kit in Calibration

Record each instrument's calibration due date, certificate number, and provider, and set a reminder before each one is due. Check calibration status before reserving a unit — so an out-of-date instrument doesn't get sent to site by mistake.

See: [Calibration Tracking](/solutions/calibration-tracking)

Every check-out, return, location change, and calibration update is logged automatically with a timestamp and the person responsible. When a client or auditor asks for the record, it's already there.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can different offices share one instrument pool?

Yes. Track every instrument's location and availability in one workspace, so any office can see what's free and where it is, and book it for a job.

### How does Shelf help keep instruments in calibration?

Record calibration due dates and certificate numbers as custom fields and set a reminder per instrument. Shelf notifies the team before a calibration is due, and staff can check status before reserving a unit. See [Calibration Tracking](/solutions/calibration-tracking).

### Is booking included for free?

Booking and multi-user access are on the Team plan. The free plan covers a single user with unlimited assets, custom fields, and reminders.

### Can field staff use it without a laptop?

Yes. The free Shelf Companion app for iPhone and Android lets staff scan, check in/out, and run audits from the field.
