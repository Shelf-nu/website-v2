# Cheqroom Alternative

Cheqroom is a well-known tool for managing equipment check-outs, especially in AV and production environments. But many teams look for a simpler, more flexible alternative that matches their workflows without heavy configuration.

Shelf offers a cleaner, modern experience designed for fast adoption, student-friendly interfaces, and real-world operational workflows.

---

## Why Teams Look for a Cheqroom Alternative

Organizations commonly switch from Cheqroom when they need:

- simpler onboarding  
- less configuration  
- faster, more intuitive workflows  
- better student or staff adoption  
- more flexible kits and bookings  
- clearer pricing  
- a platform that works across departments, not just AV  

Shelf focuses on ease and speed, not complexity.

---

## Shelf vs Cheqroom: Key Differences

### 1. Workflow-First vs Configuration-First
Cheqroom requires significant setup to match your processes.  
Shelf works with common workflows out of the box.

### 2. Modern Design & UX
Shelf’s clean UI is easier for:

- students  
- part-time staff  
- rotating crews  
- temporary users

Shelf’s booking system is built directly into asset workflows — not a separate layer.
