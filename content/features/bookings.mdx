# Bookings

Bookings let teams reserve equipment ahead of time with conflict-free scheduling. Whether you're managing cameras, laptops, tools, or shared resources, Shelf ensures availability is clear and coordination is seamless.

![Bookings overview showing reservation calendar](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/features/bookings.jpg)

---

## What Bookings Solve

Shared equipment creates coordination challenges:

- **Double bookings**: Multiple people reserve the same item for the same time without realizing it.
- **Unclear availability**: No one knows what's free, what's out, or when it's coming back.
- **Manual calendars**: Spreadsheets and paper logs drift out of sync and fail under pressure.
- **Resource conflicts**: Teams compete for the same gear, leading to delays and frustration.
- **Poor coordination across departments**: Different groups operate in silos without shared visibility.

Shelf's booking system solves these problems with real-time availability, automatic conflict prevention, and unified workflows that connect scheduling to actual handoffs.

## How Bookings Work in Shelf

### Real-Time Availability

The availability view shows exactly when each item is free. Users see open time slots at a glance—no guessing, no calling the equipment room, no spreadsheet lookups.

![Availability view showing open time slots for equipment](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/features/availability.jpg)

---

### Simple Reservation Workflow

Booking takes seconds:

1. Choose the item you need
2. Select an available time slot
3. Confirm the reservation

That's it. The system handles the rest.

![Secondary bookings view showing reservation details](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/features/bookings-2.jpg)

---

### Automatic Conflict Prevention

Shelf validates every booking in real time. If a time slot is taken, users simply can't book it. Conflicts are prevented before they happen—not discovered on the morning of a shoot. [Fabel Film eliminated double bookings with Shelf](/case-studies/fabel-film-double-bookings).

---

### Unified with Custody

Bookings and custody work together. When a reservation starts, custody transfers automatically. When equipment is returned, the system updates instantly. Scheduling and responsibility live in the same workflow.

See: [Custody](/features/custody)

---

### Kit-Aware Reservations

Users can reserve individual items or entire kits. Book a camera kit, and all its accessories come along. Shelf tracks what belongs together and keeps kits complete.

![Booking with kit showing bundled equipment reservation](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/features/booking-with-kit-alt.jpg)

See: [Kits](/features/kits)

---

### Configurable Notification Recipients

Admins control exactly who receives booking email notifications — without flooding everyone's inbox.

At the workspace level, admins configure default notification rules in **Settings > Bookings**: choose whether booking creators are automatically notified, whether all admins receive new booking broadcasts, and add an "always notify" list for people who need full visibility across every booking. On individual bookings, anyone can add per-booking notification recipients for extra stakeholders on a specific checkout.

The custodian always receives all notifications. Everyone else is configurable.
