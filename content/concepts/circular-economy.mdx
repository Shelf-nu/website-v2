# Circular Economy

The circular economy focuses on reuse, repair, shared access, and reduced waste.

---

## Principles

- Design for longevity  
- Optimize usage  
- Extend lifespan via maintenance  
- Recover value through refurbishment or recycling

See: [Preventive Maintenance](/glossary/preventive-maintenance)

---

## Why It Matters

Supports sustainability goals, reduces cost, and improves resource planning.

---

## Shelf's Role

Shelf provides usage history, maintenance logs, booking data, and dashboards to support circular practices.
