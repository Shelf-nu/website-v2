# Asset Tagging

Asset tagging is the process of attaching a unique identifier—such as a QR code—to an asset so it can be tracked, managed, and accounted for.

---

## Why Asset Tagging Matters

Asset tagging supports visibility, accountability, and operational efficiency.

---

## Asset Tagging and Shelf

Shelf uses QR-based tagging for speed, simplicity, and universal device compatibility.
