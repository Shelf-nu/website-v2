# Preventive Maintenance

Preventive maintenance consists of scheduled service tasks that keep equipment safe, reliable, and functioning correctly.

---

## Why Preventive Maintenance Matters

It reduces downtime, avoids costly repairs, extends asset life, and improves safety.

---

## Examples

- Cleaning and lubrication  
- Calibration  
- Consumable replacement  
- Firmware updates  
- Safety checks  
- Inspections
