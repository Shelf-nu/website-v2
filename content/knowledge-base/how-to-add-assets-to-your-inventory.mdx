## Adding Assets Manually

For smaller inventories, you can input assets individually. From the Assets index, click **New asset** to start adding an item.

![Assets index showing the New asset button](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/knowledgebase/add-assets-index.webp)

Fill in the asset name, category, location, image, description, and any custom fields you've set up. Click **Save** when you're done.

![New Asset form with all available fields](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/knowledgebase/add-assets-form.webp)

Leverage custom fields to capture detailed asset information specific to your business. This approach provides valuable insights and improves overall inventory management. See the [Adding additional fields to assets](/knowledge-base/adding-additional-fields-to-assets) guide for more.

## Importing Assets via Spreadsheet

For larger inventories, the bulk import feature significantly reduces manual data entry time. From the Assets index, click **Import** to upload a CSV.

![Assets index showing the Import button](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/knowledgebase/add-assets-import.webp)

Prepare your spreadsheet according to specific requirements before initiating the import process to ensure compatibility and accuracy.
