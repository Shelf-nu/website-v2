At the core of Shelf lies the concept of an **Asset**: a unique, tangible item in your inventory that you track and manage. Each asset carries its own metadata, status, usage history, custody, and any custom fields you've defined. Organising your equipment into assets is the first step toward knowing what you own, where it is, and who has it.

Two ways to get assets into Shelf:

- **[Add manually](#adding-assets-manually)** — best for small inventories, or for occasional one-off additions.
- **[Import from a spreadsheet](#importing-assets-via-spreadsheet)** — best for onboarding your existing inventory, or adding many items at once.

---

## Adding Assets Manually

![Assets index showing the New asset button](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/knowledgebase/add-assets-index.webp)

1. Click **Assets** in the sidebar
2. Click **New asset**
3. Fill in a **name**
4. Upload an image or take a photo with your phone *(optional)*
5. Choose a **Category**, **Tags**, and/or **Location** *(optional)*
6. Add a **description** *(optional)*
7. Fill in any remaining fields, including [custom fields](/knowledge-base/adding-additional-fields-to-assets) you've configured for your workspace
8. Click **Save**

![New Asset form with all available fields](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/knowledgebase/add-assets-form.webp)

Custom fields are especially useful for capturing information specific to your business — serial numbers, purchase dates, warranty dates, internal references. See [adding additional fields to assets](/knowledge-base/adding-additional-fields-to-assets) for setup.

---

## Importing Assets via Spreadsheet

For larger inventories, bulk import saves hours of manual entry. From the Assets index, click **Import** and upload a CSV.

![Assets index showing the Import button](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/knowledgebase/add-assets-import.webp)

The CSV needs to follow Shelf's expected column format (including optional columns for custom fields, barcodes, and tags). Prepare your file, preview the import, and apply — Shelf creates one asset per row.

Full walkthrough including column reference and troubleshooting: **[Importing Assets to Shelf: CSV Guide](/knowledge-base/importing-assets-to-shelf-csv-guide)**.
