At the core of Shelf lies the concept of an Asset, representing a unique, tangible item within your inventory that requires tracking and management. Each Asset corresponds to a specific piece of equipment, including its associated metadata, status, usage history, assignments, and other essential details. By organizing your belongings into individual Assets, you can better monitor their availability, condition, and deployment throughout your operations.

## Adding an Asset to Your Inventory

Follow these steps to create a new asset:

1. Click **Assets**
2. Click **New asset**
3. Fill in a name
4. **Upload an image** or **take a photo** with your phone (optional)
5. Choose a **Category**, **Tags** and/or a **Location** (optional)
6. Fill in a description (optional)
7. Fill in any other fields, or [add custom fields](/knowledge-base/adding-additional-fields-to-assets)
8. Click **Save**
