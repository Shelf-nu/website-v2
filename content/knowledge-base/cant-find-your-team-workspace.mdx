## The Most Common Cause

When you log into Shelf, you land on the last workspace you were using — which is often your **Personal** workspace. This doesn't mean you've lost access to your team workspace. You just need to switch to it.

## How to Switch Workspaces

1. Look at the **top of the sidebar** — you'll see the name of your current workspace (e.g., "Personal")
2. **Click the dropdown** next to the workspace name
3. Select your **team workspace** from the list

![Workspace switcher dropdown in the Shelf sidebar](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/knowledgebase/cant-find-workspace-1.webp)

You'll be taken directly to the team workspace with all its assets, bookings, and team members.

## I Don't See My Team Workspace in the Dropdown

If the workspace doesn't appear in the list, it could be one of the following:

- **Your invitation expired or wasn't accepted.** Ask your workspace owner to resend the invite and make sure to accept it from the email you received.
- **You're signed in with a different email.** If you have multiple accounts, make sure you're logged in with the same email address that received the invitation.
- **You signed in via SSO.** SSO users only see workspaces assigned by their administrator. Contact your IT team to make sure you've been assigned to the correct workspace group.

## Still Need Help?

If none of the above resolves your issue, reach out to **[support@shelf.nu](mailto:support@shelf.nu)** and include the email address you're using to log in.
