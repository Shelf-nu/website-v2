Now that you have set up your Shelf account, here are some instructions on how to log in.

## Log in via Username and Password

1. Go to [app.shelf.nu/login](https://app.shelf.nu/login)
2. Fill in your email address and password
3. Click **Log in**

Alternatively, you can also log in via a One Time Password.

## Log in via OTP (One Time Password)

Sometimes mail clients open links in your inbox folder, effectively breaking some workflows. An effective way to get access to your account and database is to use the OTP functionality.

1. Go to [app.shelf.nu/login](https://app.shelf.nu/login)
2. Go to "Or use One Time Password"
3. Fill in your email address and click **Continue with OTP**
4. Check your mail inbox and copy the code
5. Paste or type the code and click **Log in**
