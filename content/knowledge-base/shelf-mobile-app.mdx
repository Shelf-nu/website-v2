Shelf can be added to your mobile phone as a progressive web app for quick and convenient access to your asset management system.

## How to Add Shelf App to Your Mobile Phone

### iOS (Safari)

1. Open Safari and navigate to [app.shelf.nu](https://app.shelf.nu)
2. Tap the Share icon (square with an arrow) at the bottom of the screen
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Name the app and tap "Add"

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open Chrome and go to [app.shelf.nu](https://app.shelf.nu)
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right
3. Select "Add to Home screen"
4. Click on "Install"
5. Click on the Shelf icon on your home screen to start the application

> **Note:** The exact steps may vary slightly depending on your browser version and device settings.

## Supported Browsers

- **iOS:** Safari, Chrome
- **Android:** Chrome, Firefox, Samsung Internet, Edge

## Benefits

By adding Shelf to your home screen, you ensure your equipment database remains readily accessible. This enables inventory management, asset tracking, and operational streamlining with enhanced convenience.

Whether scanning QR codes in the field or managing your asset database in the office, having immediate access to Shelf significantly improves productivity. Contact support if you need assistance with this process.
