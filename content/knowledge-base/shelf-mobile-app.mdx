Shelf is an **asset management platform** for tracking equipment, custody, bookings, and QR-code-based operations. You can install it as a progressive web app (PWA) on any phone or desktop for quick access — no app store download required.

> **Not what you're looking for?** Shelf Asset Management is a different product from similarly-named apps on iOS and Android. If you're searching for a social media shelf, a book/movie cataloguing app, or a Pinterest companion — those are separate products by other companies.

<Callout type="info" title="Shelf Companion for iPhone is on the App Store">

The free [Shelf Companion app](/mobile-app) is **[live on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6765639874)** — an iPhone-native experience for field work: scan QR codes, run live audits, manage custody, and check bookings in and out. It pairs with your existing Shelf account. **Android is in development**; on Android or desktop, the web app (below) works in any browser and remains the full platform.

</Callout>

The PWA works great for day-to-day asset management on any device. For field-specific workflows on iPhone — barcode scanning, live audits, and custody handoffs — the [Shelf Companion app](/mobile-app) is purpose-built; the web app remains the complete platform everywhere else.

## Install on iOS (Safari)

1. Open Safari and navigate to [app.shelf.nu](https://app.shelf.nu)
2. Tap the **Share** icon (square with an arrow) at the bottom of the screen
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Name the app and tap **Add**

## Install on Android (Chrome)

1. Open Chrome and go to [app.shelf.nu](https://app.shelf.nu)
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** in the top right
3. Select **Add to Home screen**
4. Tap **Install**
5. Open the Shelf icon on your home screen

![Installing Shelf as a PWA on Android via Chrome's menu](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/670f8a79231799782f9709b2_670f8a66686edc18bece40b5_Android-min.jpeg)

> **Note:** The exact steps may vary slightly depending on your browser version and device settings.

## Install on Desktop (macOS, Windows, Linux)

Most Chromium-based browsers (Chrome, Edge, Brave) support installing web apps:

1. Open [app.shelf.nu](https://app.shelf.nu) in your browser
2. Look for the **Install** icon in the address bar (or open the browser menu → **Install Shelf**)
3. Confirm the install
4. Shelf opens in its own window and appears in your dock, taskbar, or application launcher

See also: [Add Shelf to your Dock, Taskbar, or Homescreen](/knowledge-base/add-the-shelf-app-to-your-dock-taskbar-or-homescreen) for platform-specific details.

## What the PWA Can Do

- Scan QR codes using your phone's camera
- Check assets in and out via custody workflows
- Browse and search your entire asset database
- Create and manage bookings
- View custody history, reminders, and activity logs

The PWA runs in your browser engine — it's the same Shelf you use on desktop, optimized for your screen size. No separate account, no sync issues, no app store updates to manage.
