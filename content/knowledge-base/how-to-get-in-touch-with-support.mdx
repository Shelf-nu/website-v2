We understand that issues may arise while managing your inventory, and our dedicated support team is always here to assist you. Here's how to get in touch with us when you need assistance.

If you need assistance and haven't found what you're looking for in our [Knowledge Base](/knowledge-base), don't hesitate to reach out to our support team. We're here to help regardless of your subscription plan. Depending on your plan, there may be different ways to contact us.

## Shoot Us a Mail

You can reach out to us via [support@shelf.nu](mailto:support@shelf.nu).

## Chat with Us

For quick and convenient support, we recommend reaching out to our team through our chat feature located at the bottom right corner of our website or your workspace. Our team members will respond promptly.
