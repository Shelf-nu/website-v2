Shelf's alternative barcode feature extends beyond the standard QR codes automatically generated for assets and kits. This paid add-on enables organizations to integrate multiple barcode formats into their asset tracking systems.

## Supported Barcode Types

The feature supports four barcode types:

![Method 2: Scan Existing Barcodes](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/6880d797688912bebfb7dadf_Add%20barcode%20to%20asset.jpg)

![Multiple Barcodes Per Asset](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/6880d86593fbe5d5ed2f1923_Your%20asset%20page.jpg)
- **Code128**: 4-40 characters including letters, numbers, and symbols
- **Code39**: 4-43 alphanumeric characters
- **Data Matrix**: 4-100 characters, printable at extremely small sizes
- **EAN-13**: 13-digit numeric codes matching retail product standards

## Primary Use Cases

Organizations implement alternative barcodes to:

- Maintain existing barcode systems while adopting Shelf
- Facilitate departmental preferences for different code formats
- Preserve manufacturer identification codes
- Support space-constrained labeling scenarios

## Implementation Methods

Users can add barcodes through two approaches:

1. **Manual entry**: Type codes directly into the asset barcodes section
2. **Camera scanning**: Scan existing physical barcodes using a device camera. The system automatically detects barcode type and value during scanning.

## Operational Features

- Multiple barcodes can attach to single assets with no quantity limits
- All barcode types function identically within Shelf's scanning, booking, and check-in systems
- CSV bulk import enables adding hundreds of barcodes simultaneously
- Each barcode must maintain uniqueness across the workspace

## Activating the Barcodes Add-on

The Barcodes feature is a paid add-on available on any Shelf plan. You can activate it directly from your workspace — no need to contact sales.

### Starting a Free Trial

1. Open any asset and click the **Barcodes** field area, or navigate to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Click **Start free trial** in the Barcodes section
3. If you have a payment method on file, check the consent box acknowledging that your payment method will be charged when the 7-day trial ends
4. Click **Start trial** — barcodes are immediately enabled for your workspace

The 7-day free trial gives you full access to all barcode formats across all assets.

### Subscribing After Trial

After your trial ends, you can subscribe monthly or yearly. Pricing is visible in the Barcodes unlock modal inside your workspace. Workspace owners can manage the subscription from **Settings > Subscription**.

### Non-Owner Access

If you are not the workspace owner, you will see a message prompting you to contact your workspace owner to enable the Barcodes add-on.

### Cancelling

Cancel at any time before the trial ends to avoid being charged. Manage your subscription from **Settings > Subscription**.
