Advanced index gives you much more power to interact with your asset's data on Shelf. You can read all about this feature in the [Advanced Asset Index Complete Guide](/knowledge-base/advanced-asset-index-complete-guide).

## How to enable advanced index


![Step 1) Navigate to your assets index &amp; Locate the advanced (beta) button on the lower right end of your browser window](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/6737416436fae45b48b45e91_6737414913a308e7dc3f15ba_advanced%2520mode-min.jpeg)
### Step 1: Navigate to your assets index and locate the advanced (beta) button

The advanced mode button appears in the lower right corner of the assets index page.


![Step 2) Accept the Beta Flag for Advanced mode (No risk for you, you can always toggle back to simple mode)](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/6737416436fae45b48b45e98_673741533eb4f6516a6a726f_advanced%2520mode%25202-min.jpeg)

![Step 3) You&#x27;re done. You can now explore the functionality.](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/6737416436fae45b48b45ea3_6737415dfc88277db94f9877_advanced%2520mode%25203-min.jpeg)
### Step 2: Accept the Beta Flag for Advanced mode

Click the button to accept the beta flag. There is no risk involved -- you can always toggle back to simple mode at any time without consequences.

### Step 3: You're done

Once enabled, the advanced features become available for use. You can now explore the full functionality of the advanced asset index.
