With a booking, you can reserve and check-out a selection of assets to a person (custodian) within your organization. Bookings help ensure that certain equipment is available when you or a team member needs it.

## Creating a booking

Start by going to the **Bookings** area.

1. Click **New booking** to create a booking
2. Give your booking a **Name**
3. Specify a **Booking period** by choosing a start and end time/date
4. Select a **Custodian** to whom the assets will be checked out
5. Click **Add assets** to add assets to the booking. This is only possible after configuring the booking period
6. Use the Asset manager modal to find and add/remove assets from the booking. You can use the filters and search bar to quickly find assets. Only assets that are Available during the specified booking period will show by default.
7. Click **Confirm** when you've added the assets
8. Click **Book** to confirm the booking. The status of the booking will change from **Draft** into **Reserved** or **Ongoing** depending on the current date/time and the start date/time specified under booking period
