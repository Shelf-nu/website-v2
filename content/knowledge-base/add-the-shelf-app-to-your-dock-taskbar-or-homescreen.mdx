Several methods exist for accessing Shelf more conveniently across various devices and operating systems.

## Chrome (Windows)

Windows devices like desktop PCs and Surface tablets support adding Progressive Web Apps to the taskbar.


![Chrome (Windows)](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/66aa0b95d4880f5cb5529113_6597fac2c4955670be2b1167_chrome-add-to-taskbar.jpeg)
1. Ensure you're logged into your Shelf account
2. Select the **Install** icon in the address bar (right side)
3. Click **Install** to add Shelf to your taskbar

## Safari (macOS)

macOS devices including MacBooks and iMacs can add PWAs to the dock. This functionality currently requires macOS Sonoma.


![Safari (MacOS)](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/66aa0b95d4880f5cb5529110_6564c0e36a7dcacab9a6fdee_safari-add-to-dock.jpeg)

![Safari (MacOS)](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/66aa0b95d4880f5cb552910c_6564c136cc0f17c58abdece5_safari-add-to-dock2.jpeg)

![Safari (MacOS)](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/66aa0b95d4880f5cb5529102_6564c196971fcbc1c4a8a7bd_safari-add-to-dock3.jpeg)
1. Log in with your Shelf account
2. Select **File** > **Add to dock...**
3. Choose **Add** after naming the app
4. Shelf now appears in your dock

## Chrome (macOS)

1. Log in with your Shelf account
2. Click the **Install app** icon in the address bar
3. Click the Install button
4. The app is added to your dock


![Chrome (MacOS)](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/66aa0b95d4880f5cb5529118_6565b4bfe07b931a807d6232_chrome-add-to-dock.jpeg)
## Safari (iOS + iPadOS)

iPhone and iPad users can add PWAs to their Home Screen.


![Safari (iOS + iPadOS)](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/66aa0b95d4880f5cb552911b_6565b2a6bfdb733f67612fac_safari-add-to-homescreen.jpeg)
1. Log in with your Shelf account
2. Tap the **Actions** icon at the bottom
3. Choose **Add to Home Screen**
4. Confirm by clicking **Add** and naming the app
5. Shelf appears on your Home Screen
