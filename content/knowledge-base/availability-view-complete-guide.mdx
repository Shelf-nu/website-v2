## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- Advanced Asset Index must be enabled in your workspace
- Team plan subscription (required for Bookings access)
- At least one asset with booking or reminder data

### Accessing Availability View

1. **Navigate to Advanced Asset Index** - Go to **Assets** in your sidebar, then click **Advanced Index - Explained** link, or access directly via the assets page toggle.
2. **Switch to Availability View** - Look for the **calendar icon** in the toolbar. Click to toggle between List View and Availability View. Your view preference is saved per user.

## Understanding the Interface

### Calendar Layout

- **Timeline View**: Shows dates horizontally across the top
- **Asset List**: Your assets displayed vertically on the left
- **Booking Blocks**: Visual representations of reservations and deadlines
- **Status Indicators**: Color-coded asset availability

### Booking Information Display

When viewing bookings in Availability View, you will see:

- **Start and end dates/times**
- **Custodian name** (who has the booking)
- **Booking description** (if specified)
- **Status** (Ongoing, Complete, Overdue)

## Key Features

### 1. Future Planning

- **See weeks/months ahead**: Plan resource allocation in advance
- **Identify conflicts**: Spot potential issues with custodies/bookings before they happen
- **Capacity planning**: Understand asset utilization patterns

### 2. Filter Integration

Availability View works with all Advanced Index filters:

- **Category Filters** - Example: Filter to "Camera" category to see only camera equipment availability
- **Location Filters** - Example: Filter to "Studio B" to see what is available at that location
- **Status Filters** - Example: Filter to "Available" to see only assets ready for booking
- **Custom Field Filters** - Example: Filter by "Project Type" custom field to see project-specific equipment

## Best Practices

### 1. Regular Monitoring

- **Weekly Reviews**: Check Availability View every Monday for the upcoming week
- **Monthly Planning**: Use month view for project planning and resource allocation
- **Overdue Management**: Daily check for overdue items requiring follow-up

### 2. Strategic Filtering

- **Project-Based Views**: Filter by custom fields for project-specific planning
- **Location-Specific Planning**: Use location filters for multi-site organizations
- **Equipment Type Focus**: Category filters for specialized equipment planning

### 3. Team Coordination

- **Share Screenshots**: Export views for team meetings and planning sessions
- **Booking Coordination**: Use to avoid conflicts during busy periods
- **Resource Requests**: Plan equipment requests based on availability gaps

### 4. Maintenance Planning

- **Reminder Integration**: Schedule maintenance during low-demand periods
- **Preventive Planning**: Use availability gaps for proactive maintenance
- **Deadline Management**: Track warranty expiration and certification renewals

## Common Use Cases

### Event Planning

**Scenario: Planning a 3-day conference**

1. Filter by "AV Equipment" category
2. Switch to 3-day week view covering event dates
3. Identify available projectors, cameras, and audio equipment
4. Create bookings directly from availability view

### Production Scheduling

**Scenario: Video production company scheduling shoots**

1. Filter by "Camera Equipment" and "Lighting" categories
2. Use month view to see equipment availability
3. Plan shoots around equipment availability
4. Avoid conflicts with existing bookings

### Maintenance Coordination

**Scenario: IT department planning maintenance windows**

1. Filter by "IT Equipment" category
2. Look for upcoming maintenance reminders
3. Schedule maintenance during availability gaps
4. Coordinate with existing booking schedules

### Multi-Location Resource Sharing

**Scenario: Equipment sharing between office locations**

1. Filter by specific location
2. Identify underutilized equipment
3. Plan transfers between locations
4. Coordinate with transportation schedules
