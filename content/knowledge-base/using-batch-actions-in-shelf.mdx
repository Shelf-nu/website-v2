## How to Use Batch Actions


![How to use](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/66aa0b041d5ec7a3928b839d_669e8641e17c89b31e22d447_selectassetsshelf.png)

![How to use](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/641c35b7e5057648c76fa79f/671a32467ce034862629aa5e_671a316b917bd83d0dd0fd5f_Screenshot%25202024-10-24%2520at%252013.36.56.jpeg)
### Step 1: Navigate to Assets Page

Access the Assets page in your Shelf workspace.

### Step 2: Select Assets

- Use checkboxes adjacent to each asset for individual selection
- Click "Select all XX entries" to choose all assets on the current page

### Step 3: Access Actions Menu

Click the "Actions" dropdown menu located in the top right corner after making your selection.

### Step 4: Choose Batch Actions

Available operations include:

- **Release custody**: Remove the current custodian from selected assets
- **Assign custody**: Assign a new custodian to selected assets
- **Assign Tags**: Add tags to assets in bulk
- **Remove Tags**: Remove tags from assets in bulk
- **Update location**: Change the location of selected assets
- **Update category**: Modify the category of selected assets
- **Mark as available**: Make items bookable/reservable in bulk
- **Mark as unavailable**: Make items unbookable/unreservable in bulk
- **Delete**: Remove selected assets from inventory

### Step 5: Complete Action

Follow the prompts to finalize your chosen operation.

## Tips for Effective Use

- Combine batch actions with filters and sorting to locate and update specific asset groups efficiently
- Verify your selection before performing irreversible actions like deletion
- Use batch updates for routine tasks such as reassigning equipment after team changes or updating locations following office relocations

By mastering batch actions, you maintain accurate, up-to-date asset inventory with minimal effort.
