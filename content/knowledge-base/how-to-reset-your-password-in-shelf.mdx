Keeping your account secure is essential when managing your asset inventory. This guide will walk you through the process of resetting your password in Shelf.

## Method 1: Reset from Account Settings (When Already Logged In)

If you're already logged into your Shelf account and want to change your password:

1. Navigate to [app.shelf.nu](https://app.shelf.nu/)
2. Click on your user profile picture in the bottom left corner of the sidebar
3. From the dropdown menu, select **Account settings**
4. On the account details page, scroll down to the **Password** section
5. Click the "Reset password" button
6. This will log you out and trigger the password reset flow

## Method 2: Reset from Login Page (When Logged Out)

If you've forgotten your password and cannot log in:

1. Go to the [Shelf login page](https://app.shelf.nu/login)
2. Click the "Forgot password?" link
3. Enter your email address associated with your Shelf account
4. Click "Reset password"
5. Check your email inbox for a one-time code (also check spam/junk folders if not found)
6. Enter the code to set your new password

## Important Notes

- Password reset emails are typically delivered within a few minutes
- Make sure to create a strong, unique password
- If you don't receive the password reset email, check your spam folder
- For security reasons, password reset links expire after a short period
