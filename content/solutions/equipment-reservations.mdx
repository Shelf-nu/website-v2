# Equipment Reservations

Equipment reservation software helps teams schedule, book, and manage shared gear—preventing conflicts and ensuring availability when it matters most.

![Shelf booking calendar showing equipment reservations](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/solutions/calendar-hero.jpg)

---

## What Is Equipment Reservation Software?

Equipment reservation software provides a centralized system for scheduling shared assets. It replaces manual calendars, spreadsheets, and informal coordination with structured workflows that prevent conflicts and keep everyone informed.

A modern reservation system handles availability visibility, conflict prevention, automatic check-in/check-out, and accessory tracking—all in one place. Teams can see what's available, book equipment ahead of time, and hand off responsibility seamlessly.

---

## Why Teams Need Modern Equipment Reservations

Teams that share equipment face recurring challenges:

- **Conflicting requests**: Multiple people book the same camera, laptop, or projector for the same time slot.
- **Spreadsheets that break**: Manual tracking drifts out of sync and fails under pressure.
- **Forgotten accessories**: Chargers, cables, and adapters get separated from their parent devices.
- **Manual coordination overhead**: Staff spend time resolving conflicts instead of doing their actual work.
- **Poor visibility**: No one knows what's available, what's overdue, or who has what.
- **Last-minute chaos**: Requests pile up before events, productions, or training sessions.

Modern equipment reservation software eliminates these problems with automated scheduling, real-time availability, and unified custody tracking.

## FAQs

**How does Shelf prevent double-bookings?**  
Shelf's booking system checks availability in real time. When a user selects a time slot, the system only shows open windows—preventing conflicts before they happen.

**Can I book kits with accessories?**  
Yes. Kits bundle parent assets with their accessories. Booking a kit reserves everything together, and Shelf flags missing components during check-in.

**Does Shelf support recurring reservations?**  
You can create bookings for specific time slots. For recurring needs, users can book ahead or administrators can reserve blocks of time.

**How do users check equipment in and out?**  
Users scan the asset's QR code with their phone. The system logs the transaction instantly—no paperwork, no delays.

**Can different departments have separate booking systems?**  
Yes. Workspaces let you separate inventories and bookings by department, location, or team while maintaining centralized oversight.
