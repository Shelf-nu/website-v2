# QR Code Asset Tracking

QR code asset tracking replaces sign-out sheets and spreadsheets with a single scan. Every asset gets its own QR code; anyone on the team scans it with a phone to see what it is, where it is, who has it, and its full history — then checks it in or out in seconds.

Shelf is QR-first by design. You generate codes, print them as durable asset tags, attach them to your equipment, and start tracking — no special scanner hardware and no training required. It works in any browser and in the free [Shelf Companion app](/mobile-app) for iPhone and Android.

## Why QR Codes Beat Spreadsheets and Sign-Out Sheets

Manual tracking breaks down the moment assets start moving between people, rooms, and sites:

- **No real-time status**: A spreadsheet is out of date the second someone forgets to update it.
- **No accountability**: Sign-out sheets don't tell you who *currently* has an item or when it was due back.
- **Slow lookups**: Finding a serial number, warranty date, or service history means digging through files.
- **Lost and duplicate-bought items**: Teams re-buy gear they already own because no one can find it.
- **No audit trail**: When something goes missing, there's no record of who touched it last.

A QR code turns every physical asset into a live record. Scan it and the truth is on your screen.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need a special scanner for QR code asset tracking?

No. Any smartphone camera works through the web app, and the free Shelf Companion app for iPhone and Android adds dedicated in-field scanning. There's no proprietary hardware to buy.

### Can I print my own QR asset tags?

Yes. Every asset gets a QR code automatically, and you can print them in batches as labels using the built-in Asset Label Designer. You control the size and layout.

### Does Shelf track an asset's location in real time?

Shelf records an asset's location each time its QR code is scanned — an accurate, point-in-time update. It is not continuous GPS tracking; location reflects the most recent scan.

### Can I use existing barcodes instead of QR codes?

Yes. Shelf supports Code128, Code39, DataMatrix, EAN-13, and external QR codes alongside its native QR codes, so you can keep existing labels while adopting QR workflows.

### Is QR code asset tracking free?

Yes — the free plan includes unlimited assets, QR codes, scanning, and custody for a single user. Multi-user features and bookings are on the paid Team plan.
