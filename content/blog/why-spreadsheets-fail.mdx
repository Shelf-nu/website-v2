## The Spreadsheet Trap

It starts innocently enough. You buy a few laptops, so you make a spreadsheet. Then you buy some cameras. The list grows. Suddenly, you have "Version_Final_v3.xlsx" floating around on email, and nobody knows if it's accurate.

### The problems with Excel
1.  **No History:** You can't see who had the item last week.
2.  **No Accountability:** Anyone can edit a cell and delete a record.
3.  **Not Mobile:** Trying to update a spreadsheet on a phone is painful.
