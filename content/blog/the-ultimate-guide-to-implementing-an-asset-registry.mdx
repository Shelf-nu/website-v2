## 1. What Is an Asset Registry?

An asset registry is the **single source of truth** for everything an organization owns.

It typically includes:
- identification details  
- status  
- location  
- responsible person (custody)  
- maintenance or usage history  
- notes and attachments  

A strong registry improves planning, accountability, and operational clarity.

See: [Asset Tracking](/solutions/asset-tracking)

## 2. Why Organizations Struggle With Asset Registries

Common issues:
- spreadsheets drifting out of sync  
- no consistent naming or tagging  
- unclear ownership  
- missing updates  
- poor visibility across teams
