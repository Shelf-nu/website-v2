One of the most common questions about the Shelf product is what asset tags can be generated, and we have put together a comprehensive guide to help answer this question. Our aim is to offer tags that are suitable for a wide range of assets, including monitors, headphones, roll-up banners, cables, hard drives, and even vehicles.

Asset tagging and tracking is a crucial component of efficient [asset management](https://www.shelf.nu/better-asset-management). With the increasing demand for better ways to manage and track assets, Shelf has launched an asset tagging and tracking infrastructure product that offers a simple, visual, and extendable way to manage, track, tag, and maintain assets.

One of the most common questions about the Shelf product is what [asset tags](https://www.shelf.nu/asset-tags) can be generated, and we have put together a comprehensive guide to help answer this question. Our aim is to offer tags that are suitable for a wide range of assets, including monitors, headphones, roll-up banners, cables, hard drives, and even vehicles.

Shelf offers four different tag sizes to cater to various asset sizes. 

![](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/blog/Screenshot 2023-04-19 at 19.05.28.png)

We have made it easy for users to export tags in various formats, including JPG and PNG, which are available for free. However, if you need a more advanced format like SVG, we offer it as a premium option. We believe that with these four different sizes and the option to export tags in various formats, we have covered most use cases. However, if you need a custom size or have a specific request, we encourage you to contact us via live chat.

![](https://qliecghuzfchfjwaisyx.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/website-images/blog/stickersofferedbyshelf-min.jpg)

Furthermore, we understand that some users may require custom sizes or branded stickers to suit their specific needs. That is why we have a dedicated sticker studio team ready to assist you in getting a custom quote for your needs.

In conclusion, Shelf is committed to offering the best asset tracking and tagging solutions to meet the needs of its customers. With a variety of tag sizes and export options, we aim to offer a flexible and customizable solution that meets a wide range of asset management needs. Whether you need a small, medium, or large tag, or even a custom size, we have got you covered. So, get in touch with us today to learn more about our asset tracking and tagging solutions!
