# Why We Built Availability View

Availability View came from a simple customer need: "I just want to see when this thing is free."

---

## Problems With Traditional Booking Interfaces

Calendars create clutter, confusion, and mobile friction.

---

## What Availability View Solves

- Clear availability windows  
- Mobile-friendly scanning  
- Accessory awareness  
- Simple time selection

See: [Bookings](/features/bookings)

---

## How We Designed It

We emphasized clarity, predictability, speed, and cognitive simplicity.

---

## Customer Feedback

Teams experienced fewer conflicts, faster check-outs, and smoother schedules.

---

## Use Cases

AV, engineering labs, photography, IT programs, field teams.
