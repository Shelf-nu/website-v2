**Notes for sender:**

- **Personalize the subject line and the "why this is relevant" paragraph per publication.** Generic pitches get deleted; specific ones get covered.
- **One pitch per journalist, not blast emails.** Use BCC only as a fallback for very-short-list outreach.
- **Embargo date in the subject AND the body.** Embargo break is the fastest way to lose a journalist's trust.
- **Attach the PDF.** Don't link to a public URL until embargo lifts — they'll forward to colleagues and the embargo will leak.
- **Track who got the pitch.** A spreadsheet or CRM with: journalist, publication, sent date, response date, coverage URL. This pays dividends next year.
- **Follow up once at +5 days if no response.** Then leave it.
